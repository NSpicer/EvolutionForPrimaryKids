Need help with preparing lessons on Evolution and Inheritance?

Find lesson plans designed by experts to suit you! We have consulted with primary school teachers to develop this free for all website.

The National Curriculum states that children should be taught the 3 following topics:

  1. Recognize that living things produce offspring of the same kind, but normally offspring vary and are not identical to their parents
  2. Identify how animals and plants are adapted to suit their environment in different ways and that adaptation may lead to evolution.
  3. Recognize that living things have changed over time and that fossils provide information about living things that inhabited the Earth millions of years ago.
